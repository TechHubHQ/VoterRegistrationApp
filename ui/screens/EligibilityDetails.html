Eligibility Details

Acceptable Identification

The following forms of ID are generally accepted for voter registration. Requirements may vary by state.

Driver's License
A valid, unexpired driver's license from your state
State ID Card
A valid, unexpired state-issued ID card
U.S. Passport
A valid, unexpired U.S. passport or passport card
Military ID
A valid U.S. military ID card
Tribal ID Card
A tribal identification card issued by a federally recognized tribe

Additional Information

Some states may accept additional forms of ID or have specific requirements. Check with your local election office for the most up-to-date information on acceptable forms of ID in your area.
