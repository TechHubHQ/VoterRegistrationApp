Voter Eligibility

Eligibility Requirements

Check if you meet the criteria to vote in your state.

Citizenship
Must be a U.S. citizen
Age
Must be at least 18 years old on or before Election Day
Residency
Must meet your state's residency requirements
Criminal Record
Not be currently serving a felony sentence (rules vary by state)
Mental Competency
Not be declared mentally incompetent by a court of law

Additional Information

Some states allow 17-year-olds to register if they will be 18 by the next general election. Check with your local election office for specific rules in your area.
