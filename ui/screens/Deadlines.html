Registration Deadlines

Important Deadlines

Key dates for voter registration across different states.

Standard Registration
Most states require voters to register 15-30 days before an election
Same-Day Registration
Some states offer registration on Election Day itself
Online Registration
Deadlines for online registration may differ from in-person registration
Primary Elections
Deadlines for primary elections may be different from general elections

Additional Information

Registration deadlines can vary significantly by state. It's crucial to check with your local election office or Secretary of State's website for the most accurate and up-to-date information specific to your area.
