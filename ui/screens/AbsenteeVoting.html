Absentee Voting Information

Learn about voting by mail or early voting options.

Eligibility
Eligibility for absentee voting varies by state. Some states require a reason, while others offer "no-excuse" absentee voting.
Request Process
You typically need to request an absentee ballot in advance. Check your state's specific requirements and deadlines.
Deadlines
Pay close attention to deadlines for requesting and returning absentee ballots. These can vary by state and election.
Tracking
Many states offer online tracking systems to check the status of your absentee ballot.

Additional Information

Absentee voting rules and procedures can vary significantly between states. Always check with your local election office or Secretary of State's website for the most up-to-date and accurate information specific to your area.
